# Hemp

## Scientific Name

*Cannabis sativa*

---

## Overview

Hemp is one of the oldest natural fibres used by humans. It requires significantly less water than conventional cotton and is naturally durable, breathable, and biodegradable.

---

## Key Properties

- Excellent breathability
- High durability
- UV resistant
- Naturally antibacterial
- Biodegradable

---

## Environmental Impact

Water Usage: Low

Pesticide Requirement: Very Low

Biodegradable: Yes

Renewable: Yes

---

## Common Uses

- Shirts
- Trousers
- Jackets
- Bags
- Home textiles

---

## Certifications

- GOTS
- OEKO-TEX
- European Hemp Certification
