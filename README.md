## 🌿 About

The Sustainable Fabric Library is a free community-driven resource that documents sustainable materials used in the fashion and textile industry.

Our goal is to make reliable information accessible to:

- Fashion brands
- Designers
- Students
- Researchers
- Manufacturers
- Developers
- Sustainability professionals

---

## 📚 What You'll Find

- Natural fibres
- Regenerated fibres
- Manufacturing processes
- Textile certifications
- Environmental impact
- Care instructions
- Fabric comparisons
- Glossary of textile terms
